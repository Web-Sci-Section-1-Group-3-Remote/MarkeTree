What is MarkeTree?

Trust and Certified Users

Students only can be login with the school credentials in order to access the ultimate resources on MarkeTree. Users other than students will be able to access the resources if and only if they have a referral code from students or prove their landlord's title. The students who provided the referral will take full responsibility for the referred users.

Dealing Used Products

The graduating seniors might sell their items instead of take all of them back home. Incoming students might need some used items for a better budget. you can post your used products or unwanted items on MarkeTree.

Over 300 School/Dorm Supplies

Most time, we do not need some supplies all the time. After the semester or after graduate, most materials will be useless for us. In this case, the supply is going up. Not everyone in college prefers to buy brand new products every time. Hence, the demand is here. Therefore, some used textbooks, printers, the fridge will be a good choice for incoming students.

Safe Non-Contact Transaction

All orders or transactions on MarkeTree will be processed by Venmo payment. There is no need to face to face make a deal. Especially during the pandemic, social-distancing is required.

Faster finding in over 300+ goods

Users might do not want to spend lots of time finding products. MarkeTree will provide a filter searching system for users. Everything just in one filter section.

Parnership with Union / Club

MarkeTree will behold partnership with clubs to publicize some special events, including some big sales event host by MarkeTree. In addition, users are able to host a sale event.
