What makes us Different

Unlike other marketplace websites, we offered member-only platform that gives you full experience other platform has to offered, at the same time shield you from scam and other malicious activities. We also provides event information from the campus and other authorized local organizations, so you can always look forward for interesting events.

Payments

There are multiple ways to pay your seller, through our website or other methods. Seller will have to specify which type of payment s/he accpets during listing creation. The seller can either request a cash or other types of digitalize transaction. If you decide to pay through the website, our website supports Venmo as third party payment service. COVID 19 Notice: We highly recomment digital transaction during COVID 19 Pandemic.

How to Join

As a website that aims to prevent scam and other misconduct, we implemented strict rules on our membership as followed:

If you are part of the RPI Community, you can join our website through your RCS account.

If you are NOT part of the RPI Community, you can create account through existing RPI student's referral code.

If you are a local organization, you can contact us to request an offical organization account.
